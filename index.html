Old-school parser adventure, modern conversation.

Explore a handmade fairytale kingdom, speak with its citizens, solve object puzzles, and follow the trail from the castle yard to the Chapel of Noon.

  • Typed commands: look, talk, ask, take, use, give, play, ring, and more.
  • Local LLM-backed NPC dialogue when the model is installed.
  • Animated VGA-style cutscenes, sprites, music, and ceremony finale.
  • Windows CUDA build for fast dialogue on NVIDIA RTX GPUs.
  • Mac build with bundled runtime libraries and package-local loading.
